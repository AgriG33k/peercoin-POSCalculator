Peercoin Proof Of Stake (POS) calculator

Currently it is very early days... so only basic function but you can:

  • Enter the amount of coins you sent in a transaction, the number of days that have passed and the POS difficulty to get a readout.
  • The probability of minting a block reaches its maximum after 90 days and doesn't increase after that.
  • The first column of the reward table shows you the POS reward for your custom days and for 31 days (the minimum number of days to generate a POS block).
  • The second column of the reward table then shows you the POS reward (compound interest) for minting a block 31 days after the first block (if you didn't move the coins and the POS earned from before).
  • More features to come...
